Gardens by the Bay

is a nature park spanning 101 hectares in the Central Region of Singapore, adjacent to the Marina Reservoir.

Batu Caves

is a limestone hill that has a series of caves and cave temples in Gombak, Selangor, Malaysia.

The Twin Towers

The Petronas Towers, also known as the Petronas Twin Towers are twin skyscrapers in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

Genting Highands

is a cool hill station set amidst the cool mountain air and a majestic 100 million-year-old rainforest

Me at the Merlion Park, Singapore

Merlion Park (Malay: Taman Merlion, Chinese: 鱼尾狮公园, Tamil: மெர்லயன் பூங்கா) is a famous Singapore landmark and a major tourist attraction, located near One Fullerton, Singapore.
